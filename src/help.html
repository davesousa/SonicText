Help & Documentation

Getting Started

  1. Configure your hotkey - By default, SonicText uses Ctrl+Alt+D. You can change this in Settings.
  2. Select your microphone - Choose your preferred microphone from the Settings panel.

Basic Usage

Recording Speech

  1. Press and hold your hotkey (default: Ctrl+Alt+D)
  2. Speak clearly into your microphone
  3. Release the hotkey to stop recording and process your speech
  4. Your transcribed text will appear in a floating window
  5. The text is automatically copied to your clipboard

Understanding the Status Pill

The status pill shows your current recording state:

  • Gray: Waiting for input
  • Green: Recording in progress
  • Yellow: Processing speech

Using the Tools

Three icons in the status pill provide additional functionality:

  • Optimize: Configure text optimization
  • History: View and manage past transcriptions
  • Settings: Configure application preferences

Text Optimization

SonicText can improve your transcribed text using advanced language processing. Click the optimization icon to configure:

Optimization Modes

  • Basic Mode: Fixes grammar, removes filler words, and adds punctuation. Best for quick clean-up without changing content.
  • Professional Mode: Polishes text for business communication while preserving the original message. Great for emails, reports, and presentations.
  • Concise Mode: Condenses text to its essential points while maintaining clarity. Perfect for summaries and when brevity is important.

Troubleshooting

Microphone not working

If your microphone isn't being detected or working properly:

  1. Check if your microphone is properly connected
  2. Ensure SonicText has microphone permissions in your OS settings
  3. Try selecting a different microphone in Settings
  4. Restart the application

Hotkey not responding

If your hotkey doesn't trigger recording:

  1. Check if the hotkey conflicts with another application
  2. Try setting a different hotkey in Settings
  3. Restart the application
  4. Ensure SonicText is running (check system tray)

Text optimization not working

If text optimization isn't functioning:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Try a different optimization mode
  3. Restart the application

Poor recognition quality

To improve speech recognition quality:

  1. Use a high-quality microphone
  2. Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  3. Reduce background noise
  4. Position the microphone properly (typically 6-12 inches from your mouth)
  5. Try selecting a more specific language variant in Settings
