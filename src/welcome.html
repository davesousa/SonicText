Quick Start Guide

  • Recording: Press your hotkey (default: Ctrl+Alt+D) anywhere to start recording
  • Transcription: Your speech will be automatically transcribed and copied to clipboard
  • Settings: Click the gear icon to adjust microphone, language, and other settings
  • History: Access your past transcriptions through the history panel
